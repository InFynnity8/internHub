UI/UX Design Intern

Description

Our innovative and growing company is looking for an UX & UI designer. We appreciate you taking the time to review the list of qualifications and apply for the position.

Responsibilities

  • Compile and present quantitative and qualitative findings about end-users and present to senior leadership.
  • Own and manage a specific projects around UX/UI
  • Write clear HCI specifications for ITEC for implementation
  • Provide regular progress updates and project status to the Head of User Experience & UI
  • Collaborate with cross functional teams including product management, game design and engineering ton define, design and implement fun and innovative solutions for the product direction, visuals and experience.

Qualifications for UX & UI designer

  • Experience working with tools such Photoshop, Illustrator, Balsamiq
  • Proficiency with wireframe and visual design tools
  • Functional analysis skills
  • Demonstrable UX/UI design skills with strong portfolio
  • Solid understanding of the front-end development process, including HTML and CSS
