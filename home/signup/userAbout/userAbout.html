About our company

Intern hub provides an opportunity for students from various colleges and universities to have their internships during breaks with companies and organizations. With the help of this program, numerous businesses will be able to tap into the amazing talent pool found in schools and institutions by giving organizations access to enthusiastic, university-educated young professionals who can work there while honing their professional abilities.

Mission

Our mission is to equip students with relevant skills & practical exposure (real-world experience) in the job field to help them get the best possible start in their careers. Think of a world where freedom and opportunity are abundant. Imagine living in a world where you can be certain that you will have the chance to work for and gain knowledge from businesses that you are enthusiastic about. Imagine graduating from college in a society where you are self-assured, confident, and ready to lay a claim on your place in society.

Vision

Our vision is to become a global internship hub for students across the globe to help bridge the gap between academia and work.
